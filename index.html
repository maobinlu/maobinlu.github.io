Personal information

Maobin Lu received B.Eng. in Mechnical Engineering from North China University of Technology, 2008, M.S. degree in Mechnical Engineering from Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, 2011, and Ph.D. degree in Mechnical and Automation Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, 2015. Form 2015 to 2018, he continued postdoctoral research at City University of Hong Kong. He is currently an Associate Professor in School of Automation, Beijing Institute of Technology.

Research interests

  • Networked Control: Robot Systems, Vehicles, Spacecrafts

  • Output Regulation: Linear Systems, Nonliear Systems, Time-Delay Systems
